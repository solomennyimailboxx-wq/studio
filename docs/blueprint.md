# **App Name**: Odesa Auto

## Core Features:

- Service Listings: Showcase services offered: bodywork, painting, dent repair, etc.
- Before & After Gallery: Upload and display before-and-after photos of completed projects to demonstrate expertise.
- Contact Form: Enable clients to directly message and book appointments.
- AI-Powered Visualizer Tool: A tool that allows users to upload an image of their car and visualize different paint colors or body repair options using AI.
- Customer Testimonials: Display reviews and quotes from satisfied customers.
- Geolocation: Allow users to locate your service in Odesa on a map.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Strong blue (#29ABE2) evoking trust and professionalism, nodding to the automotive industry without being clich√©.
- Background color: Light grey (#F0F4F8), providing a clean and modern backdrop.
- Accent color: Electric green (#7CFC00), drawing the user's eye for calls to action and highlights.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' sans-serif font, which combines a modern, machined, objective, neutral look.
- Use sharp, vectorized icons related to automotive services to enhance visual clarity and UX.
- Employ a grid-based layout to maintain order, enhance visual structure and facilitate navigation.
- Incorporate subtle animations such as fading transitions for loading elements.
